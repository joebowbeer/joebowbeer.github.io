Set 1

Morrison's Jig

Em jig (6/8) 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • guitar starts: dum de dum de dum (ala clash)
  • fiddle enters, plays one time thru
  • bass enters 2nd time thru
  • everyone stops after 3x+ (on signal)
  • fiddle starts again fast (1-2 1-2)
  • everyone plays 2x fast

Paddy on the Landfill

Gm reel (4/4) 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • fiddle starts after C-to-G jam, holds G, guitar plays four potatoes
  • fiddle, guitar play 1x (some restraint)
  • bass enters 2nd time thru (buildup)
  • 3x: guitar & bass play downbeat chords first 8 bars (one A)
  • then resume normal pattern
  • play 1x+ more

Pipe on the Hob / Hag at the Churn

  1. Am jig (6/8) 48-bar: A-A-B-B-C-C
  2. D jig (6/8) 16-bar: A-B
  • stagger-start Pipe on the Hob: fiddle(A)-guitar(B)-bass(C)
  • play 3x+ (note: 3-part jig, growl on C part)
  • start Hag at the Churn on all-purpose D chord
  • play D bass part next time thru A part
  • play normal chord pattern next time thru A part
  • note: 3 different accomp. for A part

Martin Wynne's / Trip to Skye

  1. Bm slow reel (4/4), 16-bar: A-B (no repeats)
  2. Bm fast waltz (3/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • guitar intro (Bm A6 Gmaj7 D A Bm)
  • play 3x, bass enters 2nd time thru
  • bass & guitar start rhythm for Trip to Skye
  • play 3x, then bass & guitar jam
  • fiddle enters, play 2x, guitar ending

Cottonwood / Ides of March Jig

  1. Am jig (6/8), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  2. Em jig (6/8), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • guitar starts Cottonwood, fiddle joins after 1x
  • fiddle starts Ides, guitar joins after 1x)
  • short ritardando to end Ides, then twiddly Scatter the Mud

Scatter the Mud

Am jig (6/8) 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • twiddly guitar and bass, then guitar plays B-part chords
  • fiddle enters on A part
  • play 3x, then stop.
  • twiddly guitar & bass intro (again)
  • organ solos on A-part chords, then stops
  • twiddly guitar & bass intro (again)
  • fiddle enters on A part
  • play 1x, then stop.

Maids of Mitchelstown / Falls of Richmond

  1. Dm aire (4/4), 16-bar: A-B
  2. Am reel (4/4), 48-bar: A-A-B-C-D-E (E=B)
  • Joe counts 3-&-4-&, everyone starts Maids together boldly
  • play 2x, end on D, slight pause
  • bass plays 2 beats to start Falls of Richmond
  • everyone plays many times: soft A, cresc. B, bass slap E part
  • repeat final E part (16 bars total), snappy ending

Grumbling & Growling

G/A reel (4/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • fiddle starts with wah shuffle A part
  • guitar, bass join on B part
  • play several times
  • bass solo, fiddle & guitar enter on B part, play 1x, end

Butterfly

Em slip jig (9/8), 24-bar: A-A-B-B-C-C
  • guitar starts with Em chord (ethereal)
  • fiddle starts on high B and winds around
  • fiddle intro ends on B-note (A string) to start tune (melody: B/E/G/E/F#)
  • 3x: fiddle & guitar trade 8 bars on A's (Em) and B's (Am)
  • fiddle returns to melody and A-B-C cycle resumes

Julia Delaney / Dinny O'Brien's / Drowsy Maggie

  1. Dm reel (4/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  2. D mix reel (4/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  3. Em reel (4/4), 16-bar: A-B(note: 8-bar B part)
  • fiddle starts with slow Julia Delaney, two A's and two Bs
  • fiddle starts Dinny's a little faster, everyone plays 3x+ (driving)
  • fiddle starts Drowsy Maggie, play faster & faster

Set 2

Wicked Jig

Em jig (6/8) 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • guitar & bass intro (chunka chunka)
  • soft accomp. on A part (chunka chunka), rousing B part
  • play 4x then bass solo then fiddle enters and repeat 2x

Rocky Road to Dublin / Tam Lin

  1. Dm slip jig (9/8), 16-bar: A-A-B1-B2
  2. Dm reel (4/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • guitar intro (4 bars)
  • everyone plays 2x, end each verse w/ 1 bar tag plus 4 bar guitar intro
  • after 3x, guitar plays 8 bar bridge
  • after 4x, guitar ends with 8 bars
  • fiddle starts Tam Lin after final note
  • guitar plays accents on 2nd A part
  • bass joins on B part
  • bass solo after 3x, stop
  • fiddle restarts faster, play 2x+

Calliope House / Sligo Creek / Cooley's Reel

  1. E jig (6/8), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  2. Bm reel (4/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  3. Em reel (4/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • play Calliope House 2x (bouncy but not too fast)
  • guitar & bass start Sligo Creek, fiddle joins
  • play Sligo Creek 3x (or until signal)
  • 4x: guitar & bass change to Cooley's chords
  • fiddle starts Cooley's Reel on B part

In from the cold

A jig (6/8), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • guitar chords, fiddle starts slow (E-F#-A)
  • fiddle plays once then guitar leads
  • play 4x, sustain last note

The Minor Spaniard / Riding on a Load of Hay / Bill Sullivan's

  1. Em jig (6/8), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  2. Em polka (4/4), 32-bar: A-A-B1-B2
  3. A polka (2/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • fiddle & guitar start and play 1x
  • 2x: everyone joins
  • play 3x then guitar starts Riding
  • play Riding 3x then fiddle & guitar start Bill Sullivan's
  • everyone joins on B part

White Summer / High Reel

  1. D guitar solo
  2. A reel (4/4), 32-bar
  • guitar solo ending in D
  • fiddle starts High Reel on B part, everyone plays 3x+ (driving)

Dinny Delaney's Slide

D jig (6/8), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • space intro, then guitar starts: dum dah dum dah dum dah dum dah 4x
  • fiddle and guitar play A part (16 bars)
  • bass enters on B as guitar switches to more chords
  • play 3x+ then stop
  • guitar plays 4 bars fast, everyone joins on B part, play 3x+ fast
  • end with 9 D's, holding last

Paddy Fahey's

Dm reel (4/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • guitar plucks dum da-da dah da-da dum da-da dah 2x
  • fiddle starts Paddy Fahey's
  • everyone plays 4x more: last time A-A is soft

Walls of Liscarroll / Road to Lisdoonvarna / Jenny on the Railroad

  1. Dm slow jig (6/8), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  2. Em jig (6/8), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  3. Am reel (2/4), 48-bar: A-A-B-C-C-B
  • guitar intro to Liscarroll after mournful bagpipe chord
  • fiddle enters, everyone plays 2x+ (ominous)
  • fiddle starts Lisdoonvarna, everyone plays 2x+ (bouncy jig)
  • fiddle starts Jenny, everyone plays 4x+

Star of Munster

Am reel (4/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • Joe counts 3-4, fiddle starts Star of Munster with pickup
  • everyone enters
  • tune ends on 2nd A part, hold final G, then 4 bar tag

Xtras

Indian Point

Em jig (6/8), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • guitar, bass play Em chord
  • fiddle plays one B part (8 bars) slowly (starts on B-note on A string)
  • everyone plays 3x

Mason's Apron

A reel (4/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • fiddle starts Mason's, everyone plays 4x+
  • snazzy fiddle & bass ending

The Mooncoin

A jig (6/8), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • fiddle starts on C# with pickup (A-B-C#)
  • 3x: fiddle plays quarter notes on A-part
  • 4x: guitar lead?
  • play 4x+, sustain last note

The Orphan / Star of the County Down

Em jig (6/8), 32-bar: A-A-B-B Am waltz (3/4) 48-bar: A-B-B (note: repeat 16-bar B part)
  • Fiddle starts with pickup (B).
  • Stagger start. Emphasize low A.
  • pause on final E, fiddle starts SotCD w/pickup (E-G-A)
  • guitar plays vamp between verses
