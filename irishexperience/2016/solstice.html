Solstice 2016

Tune List

  • Morrison's Jig (Em) - guitar starts
  • Grumbling and Growling (G/A) - fiddle starts A part, guitar & bass join on B part
  • Martin Wynne's (Bm) / Trip to Skye (Bm) - guitar chords intro
  • Rocky Road to Dublin (Dm) / Tam Lin (Dm) - guitar intro (4 bars)
  • The Butterfly (Em) - guitar starts with Em chord
  • Within a Mile of Dublin (D) - fiddle starts Dublin with pickup
  • Walls of Liscarroll (Dm) / Road to Lisdoonvarna (Em) / Jenny on the Railroad (Am) - chaos
  • Tenpenny Bit (A) / Porcupine Bit (A) - fiddle jig, then guitar & bass 5/4
  • Maids of Mitchelstown (Dm) / Falls of Richmond (Am) - Joe counts 3&4& - bass starts Falls
  • Wicked Jig (Em) - guitar & bass intro (chunka chunka)
  • Star of Munster (Am) - Joe counts, fiddle starts with pickup
  • Benton's Dream (A) - [encore] fiddle starts

Notes

Morrison's Jig

Em jig (6/8) 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • guitar starts: dum de dum de dum (ala clash)
  • fiddle enters, plays one time thru
  • bass enters 2nd time thru
  • everyone stops after 3x+ (on signal)
  • fiddle starts again fast (1-2 1-2)
  • everyone plays 2x fast

Grumbling and Growling

G/A reel (4/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • fiddle starts with wah shuffle A part
  • guitar, bass join on B part
  • play several times
  • bass break, fiddle and guitar enter on B part
  • guitar break, fiddle and bass enter on B part, play 1x, end

Martin Wynne's / Trip to Skye

  1. Bm slow reel (4/4), 16-bar: A-B (no repeats)
  2. Bm fast waltz (3/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • bass & guitar intro (Bm A6 Gmaj7 D A Bm)
  • play 3x, bass enters 2nd time thru
  • bass & guitar start rhythm for Trip to Skye
  • play 3x, then bass & guitar jam
  • fiddle enters, play 2x, bass & guitar ending

Rocky Road to Dublin / Tam Lin

  1. Dm slip jig (9/8), 16-bar: A-A-B1-B2
  2. Dm reel (4/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • guitar intro (4 bars)
  • everyone plays 1x, end verse w/ 1 bar tag plus 4 bar guitar intro
  • after 2x, guitar plays 8 bar bridge
  • after 3x, guitar ends with 8 bars
  • fiddle starts Tam Lin after final note
  • guitar plays accents on 2nd A part
  • bass joins on B part
  • bass solo after 3x, stop, fiddle restarts faster, play 2x+

Butterfly

Em slip jig (9/8), 24-bar: A-A-B-B-C-C
  • guitar starts with Em chord (ethereal)
  • fiddle starts on high B and winds around
  • fiddle intro ends on B-note (A string) to start tune (melody: B/E/G/E/F#)
  • tune is slow but driving, picks up a little speed
  • play 4x+ (improvisation)

Within a Mile of Dublin

D reel (4/4), 16-bar: A-B
  • fiddle starts Dublin reel with pickup, everyone joins
  • play 2x, then guitar break w/transp. A-part chords: E|D|E|D (32 bars)
  • fiddle enters, play 2x+, fiddle ends on D

Walls of Liscarroll / Road to Lisdoonvarna / Jenny on the Railroad

  1. Dm slow jig (6/8), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  2. Em jig (6/8), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  3. Am reel (2/4), 48-bar: A-A-B-C-C-B
  • guitar intro to Liscarroll after mournful bagpipe chord
  • fiddle enters, everyone plays 2x+ (ominous)
  • fiddle starts Lisdoonvarna, everyone plays 2x+ (bouncy jig)
  • fiddle starts Jenny, everyone plays 4x+

Ten Penny Bit / Porcupine Bit

  1. A jig (6/8), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  2. A 5/4, 16-bar: A-A-B-B
  • fiddle starts TenPenny Bit, plays 2x solo
  • fiddle ends on A string, guitar starts next beat with driving 5/4 rhythm
  • everyone plays many times, guitar plays ju-ju riffs

Maids of Mitchelstown / Falls of Richmond

  1. Dm aire (4/4), 16-bar: A-B
  2. Am reel (4/4), 48-bar: A-A-B-C-D-E (E=B)
  • Joe counts 3-&-4-&, everyone starts Maids together boldly
  • play 2x, end on D, slight pause
  • bass plays 2 beats to start Falls of Richmond
  • everyone plays many times: soft A, cresc. B, bass slap E part
  • repeat final E part (16 bars total), snappy ending

Wicked Jig

Em jig (6/8) 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • guitar and bass intro (chunka chunka)
  • soft accomp. on A part (chunka chunka), rousing B part
  • play 4x then bass solo then fiddle enters and repeat 2x

Star of Munster

Am reel (4/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • Joe counts 3-4, fiddle starts Star of Munster with pickup
  • everyone enters
  • tune ends on 2nd A part, hold final G, then 4 bar tag

Benton's Dream

A/G reel (4/4), 32-bar: A-A-B-B
  • fiddle starts, bass enters 2nd A, guitar joins on B part
  • 3x: fiddle break on B-part chords: 4 bars G, 4 bars A)
  • return to A-part after fiddle plays "real" B-part
  • play 2x+, then 2-bar tag at end
